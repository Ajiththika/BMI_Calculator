Official Health Organizations

Reliable sources for health information and guidelines:

  • World Health Organization (WHO) Obesity and overweight fact sheets.
  • Centers for Disease Control (CDC) Healthy weight information and tools.
  • NHS Live Well Advice, tips, and tools to help you make the best choices about your health.

Nutrition Basics

A balanced diet is key to maintaining a healthy BMI.

  • Eat a variety of foods: fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
  • Limit processed foods, sugary drinks, and excessive salt.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Practice portion control to manage calorie intake.

Fitness & Activity

Regular physical activity helps control weight and reduces health risks.

  • Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity a week.
  • Include muscle-strengthening exercises on 2 or more days a week.
  • Find activities you enjoy, like walking, swimming, or cycling.
